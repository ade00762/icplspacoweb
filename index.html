About Us

The Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) recently directed the recapitalisation of all banks in Nigeria. The deadline of which is April 2026.

As a consequence of this directive, 100% equity of a leading Merchant Bank in Nigeria (Project Goldman) is being sold by its shareholders.

ICPL Spaco Limited is a special purpose company registered by Iron Capital Partners Limited (Iron Capital) solely for the acquisition and recapitalisation of Project Goldman.

Mission

To build the most trusted and preferred investment bank in Nigeria.
